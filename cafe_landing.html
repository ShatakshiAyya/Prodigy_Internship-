Our Philosophy

Slow coffee, intentional mornings

We source single-origin beans from small farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala — roasted in-house every Tuesday. No shortcuts. No flavored syrups. Just coffee as it was meant to be: complex, honest, and a little surprising.
